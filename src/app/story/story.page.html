OUR STORY

The Journey to FIRST Global 2019

Team Sudan 2019 journey may have started a bit late in August due to the situation of the country but still went very well. it started when FIRST Global partner in Sudan InnoBay with the help of Mr. Faisal chose Team Sudan members after reviewing more than 60 CVs countering applicants technical and soft skills, their records and achievements in STEM field also their previous contribution in local and global competitions and exhibitions. The mentors were chosen according to their highly qualities and elementary skills in leadership, management, team work, besides the technical skills for sure. After a good study for the CVs 5 students, 1 mentor, and 1 team manager were chosen, according to their excellent records.

After the team was chosen, the team had an introduction session, and the session went well specially that team members already know each other from their past contributions. After this session, team members received all the challenge manuals and materials from their mentors. Team members had meetings every day to plan and learn more. Although we suffered through several obstacles, starting from the place to settle in, the KIT arrival and power stability. But team members were working in harmony like a beehive with positivity, hard work, self-discipline, time respect, and determination even through hard times.

After having the KIT we still have problem with no permanent venue, so Schools for gifted and talented students solved the problem temporarily, although electricity was often went out due to shortness in the country in overall.

Eventually Baylasan rented an office for the Team via Mr.Faisal and this made our work more effective and organized, Team worked from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM or more every single day except for Friday, sometimes Friday too is a working day. But due to the level of living in Sudan, Team suffered most of the times from lack of transformations, weather hot temperature (40 degrees), and electricity shut downs. And if the sky rained the next day all the streets will be filled with water and mud which make it harder to reach the team’s office. Although Team Sudan faced many difficult obstacles but that didn’t stop the team from its duties, since day one the team showed a strong well of achieving high ranks in FGC and the team didn’t missed a working day since the beginning with high team spirit and positivity

In our activities side we made many video calls with several teams and we are planning to have more since we are almost done from the robot. We made a great traditional day, we prepared for it very well in order to introduce other teams about our culture, food, and clothes. On this day we made a great sponsorship for the day including Make-up, accessories, food, venue and photography. We made steady steps in order to find an official sponsorship in order to promote STEM in Sudan persuading through meetings, calls and emails, but unfortunately no one made a positive feedback but Team Sudan is still trying and he hopes to get positive feedbacks from the sponsors next days.

Our story has not finished yet, we might updated next days with more positive news, and the challenge will be the place where a beautiful ending will be happened to us, we are very optimistic and excited, our efforts will finally pay off and we will forget all the tiredness.
