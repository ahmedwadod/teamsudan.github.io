OUR STORY

The Journey to FIRST Global 2019

Team Sudan 2019 journey may have started a bit late in August due to the situation of the country but still went very well. it started when FIRST Global partner in Sudan InnoBay with the help of Mr. Faisal chose Team Sudan members after reviewing more than 60 CVs countering applicants technical and soft skills, their records and achievements in STEM field also their previous contribution in local and global competitions and exhibitions. The mentors were chosen according to their highly qualities and elementary skills in leadership, management, team work, besides the technical skills for sure. After a good study for the CVs 5 students, 1 mentor, and 1 team manager were chosen, according to their excellent records.

FemSTEM

Encourging Women for STEM, Initiative by Team Sudan

What's FemSTEM? A lively project that highlights the struggles of women in their fields, specially STEM fields because they are always full of challenges. The highlight covers all aspects, physical mental physical, from work society and even home. The project aims to increase awareness about the problems and help solving them. This is not just a feminist program it is a project that believes both genders must be interested about with the same amount, when the level of awareness is increased we believe every person on the planet will know how to measure and balance to put people in their right places. We made a live on the 6th of October to officially launch the project, and it covered many problems faced by girls in the ancient times and nowadays.

We will be loading both lives and written contents Beside the great activities that will be held from now and during the challenge and hopefully continue after.
